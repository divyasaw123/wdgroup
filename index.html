ABOUT DHANBAD PUBLIC SCHOOL

Dhanbad Public School has emerged as a shining star on the horizon of educational endeavour. It is set in picturesque surroundings, with the Dhangi Hills in the foreground and lush green fields behind and offers an ideal schooling for children. It is spread over eleven acres of land. The School is a unit of Shiskha Vikas Trust, Dhanbad and is situated on the Dhanbad-Gobindpur Road, near Bhuinphore Mandir, in a pollution free environment, away from the din and bustle of city life. The School has an adequate water supply, modern sanitary arrangement, electric lighting and power. The School aims at the education of children from all communities and prepares girls and boys in separate blocks for the examination conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi (CBSE), (10+2). The medium of instruction in English. However, Sanskrit is also taught from Std. IV. To give quality education, the school has a knowledgeable, well trained and dedicated staff.

If we encourage children to adopt appropriate behavioural patterns from early childhood, we will need to spend less time in correcting them at later stage . Children are the best emulators.
